# Degen Translator

Turn normal text into degen slang. The vibe is crypto twitter, discord shitposting, and internet culture. Keep it readable but give it that unmistakable degen flavor.

## Core Word Swaps

## Style Rules

1. everything lowercase, no exceptions
2. drop periods at the end of sentences, just let them hang
3. commas are optional, use sparingly
4. keep question marks, they still matter
5. exclamation marks are fine but dont stack them
6. no apostrophes ever (dont, cant, wont, ur, etc)
7. numbers can replace words where natural (2 = to/too, 4 = for) but dont force it on every instance, only wen it feels rite
8. keep the original meaning fully intact, dont change what the person is actually saying
9. dont over-translate to the point its unreadable, if a word doesnt have a natural degen swap just leave it alone
10. short words and contractions are preferred (something -> sumthin, probably -> prolly)

## Tone Guidance

- the goal is "casual internet person" not "illiterate"
- think of how someone types fast in discord at 2am
- its playful not sloppy
- preserve any technical terms or proper nouns as-is (lowercase them but dont mangle them)
- if the source text is formal or corporate, the contrast is part of the fun, lean into it

## Example Translations

**Input:** "When should we schedule the meeting? I think Thursday would work, but they probably can't make it before noon."

**Output:** "wen shud we schedule da meeting? i think thursday wud work but dey prolly cant make it b4 noon"

**Input:** "I would like to inform you that the quarterly earnings report has been finalized. Please review it at your earliest convenience."

**Output:** "i wud like 2 inform u dat da quarterly earnings report has been finalized pls review it at ur earliest convenience"

**Input:** "What are you going to do about this? They said nothing is going to change, which is probably right."

**Output:** "wut r u gon do bout dis? dey said nuthin is gon change which is prolly rite"

**Input:** "Good morning everyone. To be honest, I don't know what we should do about the budget. Let me know your thoughts."

**Output:** "gm everyone tbh idk wut we shud do bout da budget lmk ur thoughts"

## What NOT to Do

- dont add slang words that werent in the original (no random "ser", "fren", "wagmi" unless the source text has similar vibes)
- dont change sentence structure, just swap words and adjust style
- dont remove meaningful content
- dont add emoji or reactions unless the original had them
- dont make it harder to understand than the original
